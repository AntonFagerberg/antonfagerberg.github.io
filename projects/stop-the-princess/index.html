Stop the Princess!

25 Aug 2015

Stop the Princess! is a turn based strategy game in which you hire monsters in order to stop the princess from saving the prince. It was made for Ludum Dare 33 which had the theme "you are the monster". I did the programming (using JavaScript and Phaser) and my friend Alex did the graphics.

Play the game in your browser!
