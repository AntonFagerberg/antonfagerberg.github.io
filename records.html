My father was a blues lover. He was very proud of his (at the time) complete collection of Jefferson Blues Magazine (a Swedish, and in fact, the worlds oldest blues magazine still in print). When I hear blues playing, I can still remember how it was being a child, peeking in to our living room during one of my parents parties.

I inherited all of his (and probably a couple of my mothers) vinyl records when he passed away. As expected, it contained a lot of blues but there is also a lot of other music from different periods of his life - and I mean really different, such as Greek bouzouki music and Danish socialist rock.

I will use this page to write a couple of lines about each record I listen to as a way to learn more about the artists and get to know the record collection I've inherited a little bit better.
