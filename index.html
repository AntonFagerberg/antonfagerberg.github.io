My name is Anton Fagerberg, this is my personal site where I show off some of the projects I've been working on and occasionally write down something that's on my mind.

I'm working as a software developer at Husqvarna Group, previously I've worked for Apple, Jayway and Bring. I recently moved back to my hometown of Jönköping after living almost a decade in Lund. I've got a MSc in Engineering (Computer Science and Engineering specialized in "Software in systems") from Lund University. I was also part of the 2017 and 2018 program committee for Øredev.

I work with Java, Dropwizard, Docker, Amazon Web Services, and some databases (DynamoDB, ElasticSearch, PostgreSQL). Before that, I worked for many years with Scala, Play Framework, Spark, Scalding, Oozie, Kafka & the Hadoop eco system. A really long time ago I was into the LAMP stack with Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP and has been known to dabble with JavaScript. I did my master's thesis with Elixir and have since been playing around with Haskell and Kotlin.
